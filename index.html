About Me

I received my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from the São Paulo State University (UNESP) in 2016.

I'm currently a Master's Student in Computer Science at the São Paulo State University under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Daniel C. G. Pedronette.

I have experience in different areas of computer science, with special interest in information retrieval, image retrieval by content, machine learning, image processing, computer vision, pattern recognition, and parallel computing.

Recently, my work "Unsupervised Similarity Learning through Cartesian Product of Ranking References for Image Retrieval Tasks" was awarded as Best Paper in the SIBGRAPI' 16 (Conference on Graphics, Patterns and Images). It was also recognized as the best undergraduate research of the São Paulo State University in 2016 and awarded as the best undergraduate work by the SBC (Brazilian Computer Society) in 2017.
